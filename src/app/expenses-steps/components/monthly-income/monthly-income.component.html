Step 1: What's your monthly income?

Let's start by getting a clear picture of your financial situation. Add up all of your sources of income, including your salary, side hustles, and any other regular payments. This will help you set realistic budgets and goals as well as help you prioritize where you want to spend your money.

Pro-tip: If your income fluctuates a lot, it can be helpful to average it out over a few months. This will give you a more accurate picture of your true monthly income.
